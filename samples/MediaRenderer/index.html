This sample demostrates the MediaRenderer with multi operations below.

Set up a dlna mediaserver, details at https://github.com/01org/cloud-dleyna/wiki.

  1. 'Scan for media renderers' button, to scan for media renderers
  2. Wait for scaning media renderers successfully
  3. 'Open' button, to open a media on the renderer
  4. 'Prefetch' button, to preload a media on the renderer
  5. 'Cancel' button, to cancel all requests has outstanding on the renderer
  6. 'Play' button, to play the renderer's current media
  7. 'Pause' button, to pause the media renderer
  8. 'Stop' button, to stop the media renderer
  9. 'Next' button, to play the next track of the renderer's playlist
  10. 'Previous' button, to play the previous track of the renderer's playlist
  11. 'Mute' button, to mute the media renderer
  12. 'setSpeed' button, to set the media renderer's play speed
  13. 'setVolume' button, to set the media renderer audio volume
  14. 'gotoTrack' button, to open the specified track number of the renderer's playlist
